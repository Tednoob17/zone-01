#### General

##### Submission Verification

###### Are all required files present in the repository? (Alison certificate or course completion screenshot, QCM screenshot, reflection)

###### Is the repository structure correct as specified in the subject?

##### AI Detection Verification

###### Has the reflection been checked using AI detection tools (GPTZero, Turnitin, or Originality.AI)?

###### Does the reflection pass AI detection, confirming human authorship?

**Note for Auditors:** Submissions flagged as AI-generated must not be validated. The reflection must be written entirely in the learner's own words.

##### Course Completion Verification

###### Did the learner submit their Alison certificate OR a completion screenshot from an equivalent open course?

###### Is the certificate or screenshot clearly readable and shows the learner's name?

##### QCM Result Verification

###### Does the QCM screenshot show a score of 70% or above?

##### Reflection Quality Verification

###### Does the reflection demonstrate genuine self-awareness about communication strengths and areas for improvement?

###### Does the reflection articulate concrete ways to improve their communication skills in daily life?

###### Is the reflection well-structured and includes specific personal examples (not generic statements)?

#### Verbal Communication Understanding

Ask: "What makes communication effective? How do you structure a clear message?"

###### Can the learner explain the structure of effective communication (intro-body-conclusion)?

###### Does the learner understand how to adapt tone for different audiences?

###### Can the learner explain techniques for speaking confidently?

#### Active Listening Understanding

Ask: "What's the difference between hearing and active listening? How do you show someone you're truly listening?"

###### Can the learner clearly differentiate between hearing and active listening?

###### Can the learner name at least three components of active listening?

###### Does the learner understand paraphrasing techniques?

###### Can the learner identify verbal and non-verbal cues that demonstrate active listening?

#### Final Validation

###### Did the learner meet all requirements (course completion, QCM 70%+, authentic reflection)?

###### Does the learner demonstrate sufficient understanding of verbal communication and active listening skills?

###### Is the learner ready to progress to the next project in Module 1: Personal Development?
