###### Is the repository structure correct as specified in the subject?

##### AI Detection Verification

###### Has the reflection been checked using AI detection tools (GPTZero, Turnitin, or Originality.AI)?

###### Does the reflection pass AI detection, confirming human authorship?

**Note for Auditors:** Submissions flagged as AI-generated must not be validated. The reflection must be written entirely in the learner's own words.

##### Course Completion Verification

###### Did the learner submit their Alison certificate OR a completion screenshot from an equivalent open course?

###### Is the certificate or screenshot clearly readable and shows the learner's name?

##### QCM Result Verification

###### Does the QCM screenshot show a score of 70% or above?

##### Reflection Quality Verification

###### Does the reflection demonstrate genuine self-awareness about strengths and areas for improvement?

###### Does the reflection articulate concrete ways to apply these skills in daily life?

###### Is the reflection well-structured and includes specific personal examples (not generic statements)?

#### Stress Management Understanding

Ask: "What stress management technique works best for you and why? How do you identify when you're becoming stressed?"

###### Can the learner explain at least two stress management techniques?

###### Can the learner identify their personal stress triggers?

###### Does the learner have a strategy for handling stressful situations?

#### Time Management Understanding

Ask: "How do you prioritize tasks when everything feels urgent? What time management framework do you use?"

###### Can the learner explain a time management framework (Eisenhower Matrix, Pomodoro, etc.)?

###### Does the learner demonstrate understanding of prioritization?

###### Can the learner identify their personal time-wasters?

#### Self-Reliance Understanding

Ask: "Give an example of when you took initiative without being asked. How do you hold yourself accountable?"

###### Can the learner explain what self-reliance means in a professional context?

###### Can the learner provide a relevant example of taking initiative?

###### Does the learner demonstrate understanding of personal accountability?

#### Final Validation

###### Did the learner meet all requirements (course completion, QCM 70%+, authentic reflection)?

###### Does the learner demonstrate sufficient understanding of all three self-management skills?

###### Is the learner ready to progress to the next project in Module 1: Personal Development?
